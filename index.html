About

Daniela Gonzalez is an Operations Manager professional and DEI champion. Her experience includes engaging board governance and program development within the nonprofit and government sectors. Daniela has led DEI initiatives to foster positive change, cultivate inclusive environments, and diversify board member recruitment. Her dedication to opitmizing business processes, DEI, collaboration, and stakeholders has led to her increasing overall organizational success.
She spends her free time hiking, trail running, throwing pottery, and watching films.

Her website is titled 'Diverse Fires' because it symbolizes the transformative energy of kiln fires, giving life to unique ceramic art. Beyond the kiln, it embodies her dedication to breaking down inequities, fostering a diverse and inclusive environment, and kindling positive change in organizations and beyond.



Driven by a passion for making a positive impact, she is currently contributing her skills and dedication to fostering inclusive and effective environments in the military sector.

Daniela is also a contracted workshop facilitator with the Long Beach based DEI consulting firm, Willing Observers.

Ceramics

Daniela began her ceramics journey during 2020 pandemic. This coincided with her undergraduate studies, where she found solace and inspiration in her first handbuilding course. As she transitioned into graduate school, her passion for ceramics deepened, leading her to learn the art of wheel throwing. While Daniela solidified her commitment to understanding, supporting, and building communities she also discovered a profound connection between her love for ceramics. The wedging table and wheel became not just tools for crafting clay but symbolic spaces where patience and commitment translated into beautiful forms. Daniela has established her home studio. Daniela is on a mission to define her unique artistic style through clay forms. She aims to share a narrative of clay and community, where each piece tells a story of patience, commitment, and the beauty that emerges when diverse elements come together.
